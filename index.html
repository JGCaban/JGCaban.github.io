About Me

My name is Jose Caban and welcome to my site. I am a Software Developer, Emergency Manager, and a US Navy for 13-years. Grew up in a small community in San Sebastian, Puerto Rico. At age 18, I decided that life on an island was not for me and joined the US Navy with little to no practice speaking English. Majority of my time in the Navy was as Military Police as a Small Arms & Crew Served Weapons Instructor. During that time I had the priviledge to visit over 15 different countries and lived in Japan for 8 years. After being dicharged from from the military, I landed in Indiana where I had immediate family. I attended the University of Indianapolis to study Emergency Management and worked as an Operational Specialist for Simon Property Group for a year. After that I decided that I needed some tech skills. I have always had an affinity towards tinkering with things and coding certainly helps that need. Thanks you for visiting and please contact me if you would like to know more.
